The Santa Monica Fitness Club is all about getting people fit while keeping it interesting, fun and challenging. It is an accessible fitness regimen designed to produce measurable improvements in function and performance. No hype, no gimmicks and no fads; only really hard workouts from a variety of sources set up to get more out of the time you spend training.

Running, boxing, kettlebell swinging, sandbag lifting, plyometrics and much more are utilized to make you as fit as you can be. Most movements can be scaled to accommodate varying abilities, so whether your as fit as a decathlete or a couch potato, you can still get a great workout!

The classes are run on a “pay what you want” system so there’s no financial obligation to you. You just workout and pay what you think it was worth! Come down and be a part of the SaMo Fitness Club today!

Pay what you want pricing

After the session you decide how much to pay based on the service you recieved. No packages or contracts. That way you will allways get 100% of what you pay for!
